The Eclipse Modeling Framework Technology project was initiated to incubate new technologies that extend or complement EMF.

Because EMFT is an incubator, it does not have annual coordinated releases; however, individual components may choose to participate in Eclipse's annual release train or to align its development plan with that of another Modeling project such as EMF or MDT.

Contributions are currently being offered relating to model comparison, JCR management, search, persistence and more.
