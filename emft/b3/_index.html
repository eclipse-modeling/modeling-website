b3

The purpose of the b3 project is to develop a new generation of Eclipse technology to simplify software build and assembly.

Eclipse b3 consists of two parts; the b3 engine, and the b3 site aggregator.

Status

The b3 aggregator is fully functional and available on the b3 update site. The b3 engine is under development and can be obtained from the b3 SVN repository.

Incubation

b3 is in the Incubation Phase.
