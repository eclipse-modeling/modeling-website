The project is proceeding well and is scheduled to release version 1.0 as part the Callisto release, scheduled for the end of Q2 2006. Refactoring and enhancement of the runtime contribution is underway, as is the development of the tooling of GMF. A tutorial is posted to illustrate GMF's capabilities and to solicit feedback from the community. It is expected that given the popularity of the project, the strong team in place, and the reasonable goals outlined in the project plan, GMF will successfully exit Incubation in Q2 of 2006.
