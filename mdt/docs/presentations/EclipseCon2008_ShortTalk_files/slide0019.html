Components – UML2
•UML2 is an EMF-based implementation of the Unified Modeling Language 2.x OMG specification for the Eclipse platform
•
•UML2 provides
wa useable implementation of the metamodel to support the development of modeling tools
wa common XMI® schema to facilitate interchange of semantic models
wtest cases as a means of validating the specification
wvalidation rules as a means of defining and enforcing levels of compliance
