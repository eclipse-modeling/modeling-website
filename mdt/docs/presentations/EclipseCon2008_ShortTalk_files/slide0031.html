Components – SBVR
•SBVR is a new component aimed at providing a metamodel implementation and sample tools based on the adopted Semantics of Business Vocabulary and Business Rules (SBVR) OMG specification
•
•SBVR will provide
wan open source "reference" implementation of the SBVR specification
wan EMF-based foundation on which business vocabulary and business rules modeling tools can be built
wa basis for integrating and interchanging artifacts between business vocabulary and business rules tools
wa forum for engaging the community in validation of the SBVR specification
